The Push-up grid

The Push-up grid is an idea from Bobby Maximus of Gym Jones. The idea is that - without time, money, equipment or a gym - you can still workout. Even on busy days.

How you do it:

  • Step 1:

    Draw a grid on a piece of paper, like a tic-tac-toe box You can make as many boxes as you like. The more boxes, the harder the workout.

  • Step 2:

    Do pushups. With each set, fill in a box. Your goal is to fill out the boxes with sets of pushups.

Or: you can use this app for the grid instead of a piece of paper, get started and track your progress.
Have fun!
